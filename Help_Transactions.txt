GrowCery user help file
========================================

To veiw the transactions preformed by a specific
teller you must: 

1.) Select a teller by clicking on the desired teller in 
    the table labled "Accounts".
2.) Click on the button labled "Show Transactions".
3.) You can then veiw the details of specific transactions
    by clicking on the transaction in the table labled 
    "Transactions".
4.) The information details regarding the specifc 
    transaction will be in the box labled 
    "Transaction Details".
